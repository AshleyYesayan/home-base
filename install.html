Home Base

Your household on autopilot — tasks, meals, groceries and reminders. It installs straight from the web. No App Store, no download.

On iPhone or iPad

  1. Open this page in Safari (not Chrome — Apple only allows installing from Safari).
  2. Tap the Share button — the square with an arrow pointing up.
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add. Home Base is now an icon on your home screen.
This step matters. Apple only allows notifications and the barcode scanner to work once Home Base is on your home screen. In a browser tab, they won't.

On Android

  1. Tap Install Home Base below.
  2. If nothing appears, open the browser menu (⋮) and choose Install app or Add to Home screen.

Share it with someone

Send it to anyone in your household. They follow the same steps, sign in, and you'll all see the same tasks, pantry and meal plan — updating live on every phone.
