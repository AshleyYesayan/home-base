Privacy Policy

Home Base · Last updated 12 July 2026

Home Base is a household-management app. This policy explains exactly what it stores, who it shares data with, and how to get your data deleted. It is written to be read, not to be survived.

What we collect

DataWhy
Your email address and display nameTo sign you in and to show other members of your household who a task is assigned to.
Your household content — tasks, upkeep reminders, pantry and freezer items, meal plans, recipesThis is the product. It is what the app exists to store.
Push notification subscription (a device token)Only if you tap “Notify me”. Used to send you task and restock notifications. Nothing else.
Local preferences on your device (dashboard order, chosen filters, remembered barcodes)Stored in your browser, not on our servers.
We do not collect: payment details, location, contacts, health data, advertising identifiers, or analytics about your behaviour. We do not run ads. We do not sell or rent your data to anyone, for any purpose.

Who can see your data

Only the members of your household. Every table is protected by row-level security, so a request for another household’s data is rejected by the database itself, not merely hidden by the app.

Services we rely on

ServiceWhat it receives
SupabaseHosts the database and handles sign-in. Your account and household content live here.
GitHub PagesServes the app itself. It receives no household data.
Google Sign-InIf you sign in with Google, Google confirms your identity and gives us your email and name.
Open Food FactsWhen you scan a barcode we send that barcode to look up the product name. No personal data is sent.
Amazon and InstacartOnly if you tap a shopping link. You then leave Home Base and their privacy policies apply. Amazon links carry an affiliate tag, which may earn us a commission at no cost to you.

Deleting your account

You can delete your account and all of its data from inside the app, at any time, without emailing anyone. Deletion is immediate and permanent: your account, your tasks, your pantry, your meal history and your push subscriptions are removed. If you would rather we do it, write to ashley@onevillage.io and we will action it within 30 days.

Children

Home Base is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, we will say so in the app rather than quietly updating this page.
