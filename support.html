Common questions

I’m not getting notifications on my iPhone.

Apple only delivers web notifications to apps installed on the Home Screen, not to a Safari tab. Open Home Base in Safari, tap the Share button, choose Add to Home Screen, open it from there, then tap Notify me and allow notifications.

The barcode scanner won’t open.

It needs camera permission. If you declined it once, your browser will not ask again — re-enable the camera for this site in your browser’s site settings.

I scanned something and it wasn’t recognised.

The product database we use covers food well and household goods poorly. When a barcode isn’t found, just type the name once — Home Base remembers that barcode, and next time you scan it the count goes up on its own.

Can I share a household with someone?

Yes. Use the Invite button in the header. Everyone in a household sees the same tasks, pantry and meal plan, and tasks can be assigned to any member.

How do I delete my account?

Inside the app, from your account menu. It is immediate and permanent — your account and all of your household data are removed. See the Privacy Policy for detail.

Something looks wrong / I found a bug.

Email ashley@onevillage.io with a screenshot if you can. It genuinely helps.
